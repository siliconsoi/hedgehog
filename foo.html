Getting There Haven is located along beautiful and serene surroundings at Hua Hin - Cha Am beach, attached to a public inner road which branches from Phetchkasem Road km. 222 and km. 223 (opposite to Stamford University and Palm Hill Golf Resort and Country Club). The inner road is situated about 300 m. before Hua Hin Airport Tunnel, or 10 minutes to Hua Hin Market. Take the inner road until Chala Samut Road, then turn left, go further for 500 m. and you will see Haven- Hua Hin on the left. The trip takes about 2.5 hour by car from Bangkok.
